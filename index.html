Our Story

Traditionally cured, slowly air-dried — the old way.

Excellent Venison crafts premium biltong, droëwors and cured meats the traditional South African way — properly seasoned, patiently air-dried, no shortcuts. From our facility in Nigel, Gauteng, we supply office canteens, convenience stores, tuck shops, butcheries and supermarkets, alongside direct orders from the public. From classic beef to lean, characterful game like kudu and springbok, every batch is made to be the one that disappears first.

  • Proudly South African
  • Traditionally cured & air-dried
  • Free delivery in Pretoria, Centurion & Gauteng
